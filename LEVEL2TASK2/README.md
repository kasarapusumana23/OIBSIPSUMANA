# 🌟 Tribute Page – Sundar Pichai

## 🎯 Project Overview

This project is a responsive Tribute Page dedicated to **Sundar Pichai**, the CEO of Google and Alphabet Inc. The webpage highlights his inspiring journey, achievements, leadership qualities, and contributions to the technology industry through an elegant and visually appealing design.

The project aims to honor one of the world's most influential technology leaders while demonstrating web design and development skills.

## 🌟 Project Highlights

### 👨‍💼 About Sundar Pichai

Sundar Pichai is an Indian-American business executive who serves as the CEO of Google and Alphabet Inc. His leadership has played a significant role in shaping products used by billions of people worldwide.

### 🚀 Career Journey

* Graduated from IIT Kharagpur
* Pursued higher education at Stanford University
* Joined Google in 2004
* Led the development of Google Chrome
* Became CEO of Google in 2015
* Became CEO of Alphabet Inc. in 2019

### 🏆 Major Achievements

* Led the development and growth of Google Chrome
* Expanded Android's global reach
* Guided Google's innovation in AI and Cloud Computing
* Recognized among the world's most influential leaders

### 💬 Inspirational Quote

> "Wear your failure as a badge of honor."
